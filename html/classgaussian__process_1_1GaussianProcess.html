Creates Gaussian Process object.

Gaussian Process Objects are used for data regression. They are defined by there Hyperparameters and the data used to train the process. See http://www.gaussianprocess.org/gpml/ for further informations.

Parameters
x_trainnumpy_matrix: Input of Traning Data
y_trainnumpy_matrix: Output of Training Data
parameter_sig_nfloat: Hyperparameter defining output noise
parameter_lfloat: Hyperparameter defining length scale
parameter_sig_ffloat: Hyperparameter defining process noise
